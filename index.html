Why I decided to take IDP at Fanshawe

I have taken a variety of art courses over my artistic career, and worked with many kinds of physical mediums. But the most exciting classes were the ones where I got to work with some basic HTML and CSS, and some digital 3D work. I enjoyed them so much, that I decided to make it my career, to blend my artistic pursuits with the unique possibilites of these technologies.
